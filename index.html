Software Engineer & Data Scientist

I am a passionate software engineer and aspiring data scientist currently pursuing a Master's in Information Technology with a major in Data Analytics at Griffith University. With a robust foundation in software development, I am dedicated to leveraging data-driven solutions to solve complex problems.

My career journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Mehran University of Engineering and Technology, where I graduated with distinction, ranking 4th in my class. This academic achievement fueled my ambition to dive deeper into the world of data science and analytics.

My current role as a Data Intern at Australian Community Media has provided me with hands-on experience in executing SQL queries, optimizing query performance, and developing automated data workflows using Apache Airflow. I have also contributed to significant projects such as migrating data pipelines from UA to GA4.

My career aspirations revolve around becoming a leading expert in data science and analytics. I aim to harness the power of big data to drive innovation and efficiency in various industries. By continuously honing my skills in machine learning, statistical analysis, and backend development, I strive to develop solutions that can transform data into actionable insights.
