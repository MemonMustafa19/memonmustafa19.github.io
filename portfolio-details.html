Critical Analysis of WIL Placement

During my WIL placement at Australian Community Media (ACM), I worked on several projects aimed at improving customer engagement and subscriber retention. This included developing and maintaining data pipelines, optimizing SQL queries, and implementing machine learning models for churn prediction. A critical reflection on my experience revealed that while I successfully contributed to several key projects, there were areas where I could have improved, such as time management and proactive communication with stakeholders. For instance, the initial stages of the Viafoura data pipeline project faced delays due to a lack of thorough initial planning. By adopting a more structured approach to project management and enhancing my technical skills through continuous learning, I was able to overcome these challenges and deliver effective solutions.
